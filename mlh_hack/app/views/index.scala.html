Features

Authors

You may search for an author in the top right hand corner and retrieve information about them as well as a list of the papers they have written. Any author featured on Web of Science may be looked up. You can additionally access specific papers directly from here.

Articles

Whether you are looking up a particular author and want to see the papers they have written, or if you want to look up a specific paper, you can see basic information about this paper. This includes who the paper has been written by, the year it has been published in, the location it has been published from and the abstract.
